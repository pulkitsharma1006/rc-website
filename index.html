Why Fuel Monitoring is the need of the hour.

Fuel makes up 30% of trucking costs, directly influencing freight rates and profits. Sudden price shifts impact logistics, while 40% of fleet fuel is lost to theft, affecting bottom lines. A Fuel Level Sensor is the most effective solution to prevent theft. Integrated with vehicle telematics, it tracks fuel usage, detects anomalies, and enables proactive measures to safeguard fuel and optimize costs.
